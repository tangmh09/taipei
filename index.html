Taipei in Mid-October

Day 1: Arrival & Ximending

Arrival
15:00
Arrive at Taoyuan International Airport (桃園國際機場). Take the Airport MRT to Taipei Main Station (台北車站) and check into your hotel.
17:00
Settle into your hotel and freshen up after the flight.
18:30
Head to Ximending (西門町), the vibrant youth shopping district full of neon lights and pop culture.
19:30
Eat street food! Grab the famous Ay-Chung Flour-Rice Noodle (阿宗麵線) and a massive Hot-Star Large Fried Chicken (豪大大雞排).

Day 2: City Icons & Views

Sightseeing
07:00
Early breakfast at the Michelin Bib Gourmand Fuhang Soy Milk (阜杭豆漿). Go early to beat the massive queue!
09:00
Walk to Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall (中正紀念堂) to see the striking architecture and the changing of the guard ceremony.
11:00
Explore Yongkang Street (永康街), an area famous for its boutique shops and incredible food scene.
12:30
Lunch at the original Din Tai Fung (鼎泰豐) on Xinyi Road (expect a wait), or try a local beef noodle soup spot nearby.
14:30
Head to the modern Xinyi District to visit the observatory at Taipei 101 (台北101).
16:30
Hike up Elephant Mountain (Xiangshan - 象山) (about 20-30 mins of stairs) for the best sunset and night views of Taipei 101.
19:00
Dinner at Raohe Street Night Market (饒河街觀光夜市). Don't miss the famous black pepper buns (胡椒餅) near the temple entrance.

Day 3: History & Hot Springs

Culture & Relax
09:30
Visit the National Palace Museum (國立故宮博物院) to see thousands of years of imperial Chinese artifacts (Closed Mondays).
13:00
Head back towards Shilin Station (士林站) for lunch, then take the MRT to Xinbeitou (新北投).
14:00
Explore the steaming Beitou Thermal Valley (地熱谷) and the beautiful wooden Beitou Hot Spring Museum (北投溫泉博物館).
16:00
Relax in the healing waters. You can visit the cheap public outdoor baths (swimsuit required) or book a private bathhouse room.
18:30
Take the MRT to Shilin Night Market (士林夜市), the largest and most famous night market in Taipei, for dinner and arcade games.

Day 4: Day Trip to Mountain Towns

Day Trip
09:00
Take a TRA train from Taipei Main Station (台北車站) to Ruifang Station (瑞芳車站), then transfer to the Pingxi Line (平溪線).
11:00
Arrive in Shifen (十分). Walk to the impressive Shifen Waterfall (十分瀑布), often called the Niagara Falls of Taiwan.
13:00
Paint your wishes and release a paper lantern directly on the active train tracks at Shifen Old Street (十分老街).
15:00
Take a bus or taxi to Jiufen Old Street (九份老街). Explore the narrow, winding alleys full of red lanterns, sweet taro balls, and souvenirs.
17:00
Have traditional tea at the A-Mei Tea House (阿妹茶樓) (said to inspire Spirited Away) and enjoy coastal views as the sun sets.
19:30
Take the direct bus (1062 or 965) back to Taipei.

Day 5: Temples & Old Taipei

Heritage
09:30
Visit Longshan Temple (龍山寺), one of Taiwan's oldest and most atmospheric temples, filled with chanting and incense.
10:30
Walk nearby through the preserved brick buildings of the Bopiliao Historical Block (剝皮寮歷史街區).
12:30
Take the MRT to Huashan 1914 Creative Park (華山1914文化創意產業園區), an old winery turned into art spaces and cafes. Great for lunch.
15:00
Head to the Dadaocheng (大稻埕) district and walk along Dihua Street (迪化街), famous for Chinese medicine shops, fabrics, and hip coffee shops hidden in old courtyards.
18:00
Enjoy a fantastic dinner at Ningxia Night Market (寧夏夜市). It is smaller but incredibly highly rated for food quality, specifically taro balls and oyster omelets.

Day 6: Tea Mountains & East District

Nature & City
09:30
Take the MRT down to Taipei Zoo (臺北市立動物園). You can do a quick visit to see the pandas, or just head straight to the gondola.
11:30
Ride the Maokong Gondola (貓空纜車) up into the mountains (try to grab a crystal cabin with a glass bottom).
13:00
Enjoy a slow, relaxing lunch at a traditional tea house in Maokong (貓空), pairing local tieguanyin tea with tea-infused foods overlooking the city.
15:30
Head back to the city. Visit Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall (國父紀念館) or relax at Songshan Cultural and Creative Park (松山文創園區).
18:00
Shop and explore the East District (Dongqu - 東區), full of trendy fashion boutiques, cafes, and bars.
19:30
Grab dinner at Linjiang Street (Tonghua) Night Market (臨江街夜市) for a very local, authentic vibe to end your last full day.

Day 7: Souvenirs & Departure

Departure
09:30
Have a relaxing morning stroll through Daan Forest Park (大安森林公園).
11:00
Grab the original boba milk tea (珍珠奶茶) from Chun Shui Tang (春水堂) and pick up some boxes of Pineapple Cakes for souvenirs.
13:00
Do any final underground mall shopping at Taipei Main Station (台北車站).
15:00
Take the Airport MRT back to Taoyuan International Airport (桃園國際機場) for your flight home. Safe travels!
