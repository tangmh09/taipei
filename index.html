Practical Tips

EasyCard

Buy at the airport. Essential for MRT, buses, and convenience store purchases.

Convenience Stores

7-Eleven & FamilyMart are everywhere. Great for cheap coffee, ATMs, and tickets.

Comfortable Shoes

You will walk on concrete and climb stairs. Bring broken-in sneakers.

Trash Cans

Public bins are rare. Carry a small plastic bag until you find an MRT station bin.

Enjoy your adventure in Taiwan!
